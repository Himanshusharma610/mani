The Day We Grew Closer

Aaj ka din hamara bohot hi special tha — aur iska reason ye tha ki aaj hamne starting ke 2 dino ki tarah na overlove kiya, na hi video call pe chipke rahe. ✨ Aaj ka din bilkul alag tha, bilkul peaceful aur real.

Hum dono bas shanti se ek dusre ke saath baithe rahe, dheere-dheere baatein karne lage — apni zindagi ki chotiyon badi sab cheezein share kiin. 🥰 Apne Past Present Future ke baare mein khul ke baat ki, apne sapne bataaye, apne darr bataaye, aur ek dusre ko samjha.

🌙 · ✨ · 💫 · ✨ · 🌙

Sabse pyari chiz thi ki usne aaj apni dono behnon se milwaya video call par! 👩‍👧‍👧 Uski poori family bohot hi achi thi — bohot open-minded, supportive, aur ek dusre ke liye caring. Aunty bhi bohot chill thi aur unka pyaar bilkul real tha. Ye dekh ke dil khush ho gaya ki uski family itni sundar hai. 💕

Fir hamne ek dusre ke mann mein chal rahe alag alag thoughts aur overthinking ko dur kiya — jo darr tha, jo confusion tha, sab kuch khul ke bola. Aur ussi waqt hamne ek dusre ke saath ek bohot strong aur deep bond bana liya. 🔗💖

Ek aur interesting baat — logo ko toh trust hi nahi tha ki hum dono ab relationship mein aa chuke hain! 😜 But who cares, because hamari duniya hai aur hum apni duniya mein bohot khush hain. 🌍❤️

🩷 · 🦋 · 💗 · 🦋 · 🩷

Aur ab sabse important baat — wo aaj pink t-shirt mein haaaye kya lag rahi thi! 😍🩷 Nazrein hatt hi nahi rahi thi uspe... itni sundar, itni pyari — bas dekhte reh jaao! And most fun part? Usse irritate karna mujhe bohot zyada achha lag raha tha 😂🤭 — uska gussa karna, fir cute sa muh banana... everything was just perfect.

Aur fir thoda thoda karke hum dono ek dusre ke liye bohot deep ho gaye — jaise ek aisi feeling jo words mein describe nahi hoti, bas dil se mehsoos hoti hai. 💝 Ye din yaad rahega hamesha, because aaj hamne sirf pyaar nahi kiya... aaj hamne ek dusre ko sachchi tarah se samjha. ✨
